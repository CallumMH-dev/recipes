Potato and leak soup

Healthy potato-leak soup for vegetarians

simple, straight-to-the-point and genuine.

    Ingredients

  • ¼ cup olive oil, divided
  • 2 leeks, chopped
  • 1 (32 fluid ounce) container vegetable broth
  • 1 ¾ cups water
  • 2 pounds potatoes, cut into cubes
  • ⅜ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
